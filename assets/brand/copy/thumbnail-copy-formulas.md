# Finplaza Thumbnail Text Cheat-Sheet

Bold. Energetic. Scroll rok dene wali. India ki finance audience ke liye banayi gayi, aur compliance ke hisaab se tuned.

---

## 1. The 6 Hook / Word-Count Formulas

> Rule of thumb: thumbnail text ek **headline hai, sentence nahi**. Curiosity + ek concrete subject = click.

### Formula 1 — The Number Shock (3-4 words)
`[BADA NUMBER] + [SUBJECT]`
- **₹50,000 CREDIT LIMIT**
- **0% EMI TRICK**
- **5 SAVINGS GALTIYAN**

*Kyun chalta hai:* ek hard number proof jaisa padhta hai. Number ko gold accent mein rakho (legibility rules dekho).

### Formula 2 — The Secret / Reveal (2-3 words)
`[CHHUPA HUA] + [TOPIC]`
- **CHHUPE CC CHARGES**
- **BANK KA SECRET RULE**
- **YE TRAP HAI** (card image ke saath)

*Kyun chalta hai:* insider knowledge ka feel deta hai, bina koi claim kiye.

### Formula 3 — The Bold Question (3-5 words)
`[KYUN/KAISE/KAUNSA] + [SUBJECT] ?`
- **KAUNSA CARD BEST?**
- **CIBIL KYUN GIRA?**
- **SAVINGS YA FD?**

*Kyun chalta hai:* jis sawaal ka jawaab viewer chahta hai = ek open loop. `?` saara kaam karta hai — usko bada rakho.

### Formula 4 — The Versus / Comparison (3 words)
`[A] vs [B]`
- **SBI vs HDFC**
- **CREDIT vs DEBIT**
- **UPI vs CARD**

*Kyun chalta hai:* built-in tension. Do logos/images text ke dono taraf ho to best chalta hai.

### Formula 5 — The Warning / Stop (2-3 words)
`[RUKO/BACHO/SAVDHAAN] + [SUBJECT]`
- **RUK JAO!**
- **YE CARD GALAT HAI**
- **CIBIL ALERT**

*Kyun chalta hai:* loss-aversion. Kam use karo taaki channel alarmist na lage.

### Formula 6 — The Step / List Promise (3-4 words)
`[NUMBER] + [STEPS/TARIKE/RULES]`
- **3 STEP SE 800**
- **7 SAVINGS RULES**
- **4 CARD HACKS**

*Kyun chalta hai:* finite, digestible, low-effort promise. List number gold-number trick ke saath perfectly jamta hai.

## 2. Legibility Rules (Phone-Size First)

Zyadatar viewers thumbnail ko ek postage stamp ke size mein dekhte hain. Worst case ke liye design karo.

| Rule | Target |
|------|--------|
| **Max words** | screen par **3-4 words**. Hard cap 5 par. Isse zyada chahiye to tum title likh rahe ho, thumbnail nahi. |
| **Font size** | Text ki height kam se kam **thumbnail height ki 1/3** honi chahiye. Agar 5-inch screen par readable nahi, to fail. |
| **Word count test** | Phone par arm's length se **1 second ke andar** padh pao. |
| **Contrast** | Dark area par light text ya light area par dark text — kabhi bhi busy image ke upar seedha text nahi. |
| **Stroke / shadow** | Ek thick **outline (stroke) + drop shadow** lagao taaki text kisi bhi background par survive kare. |
| **Color count** | **Max 2 text colors**: ek base (white/black) + ek accent. |

### The Gold-Number Trick
Sabse important **number ya word ko GOLD** (ya bright yellow `#FFD200`) banao, baaki sab white ya black rehne do.

- **₹50,000** SALARY → **PERFECT CARD** *(number par gold)*
- CIBIL **800** *(800 par gold)*
- BACHAO **₹1 LAKH** *(amount par gold)*

*Kyun:* aankh pehle single gold element par lock hoti hai, phir baaki padhti hai. Ye instant visual hierarchy banata hai aur signal deta hai "yahi payoff hai." Ek thumbnail mein ek hi gold element — kabhi do nahi, warna trick mar jaati hai.

### Quick contrast pairings (Finplaza palette)
- **White text + gold number** ek deep navy/black panel par — credit cards/banking ke liye default.
- **Black text + gold number** ek bright background block par.
- **Red accent** sirf WARNING formula ke liye — red ko reserve rakho taaki meaningful rahe.

## 3. Compliance-Safe Do / Don'ts

Finance content mein real regulatory risk hota hai. Thumbnail ek public claim hai — usko waise hi treat karo.

### Do
- **Do** curiosity, questions, aur ranges use karo ("UP TO", "GALTIYAN", "TRICK") promises ke bajaye.
- **Do** numbers ko video ke actual content se joda hua rakho (ek real limit, ek real fee, ek real example jo andar dikhaya gaya ho).
- **Do** opinions ko opinion ki tarah frame karo ("MERA PICK", "MAIN YE CHUNUNGA") na ki directive ki tarah.

### Don't
1. **No "GUARANTEED" / "100%" / "RISK-FREE" / "ASSURED RETURNS"**
   - Galat: **GUARANTEED 30% RETURN**
   - Fix: **YE 30% WALI GALTI** ya **30% MIL SAKTA HAI?**
2. **No buy/sell ya "INVEST NOW" calls to action**
   - Galat: **YE STOCK KHARIDO** / **ABHI BECHO**
   - Fix: **KHARIDNA CHAHIYE?** / **YE WORTH HAI?**
3. **No invented ya unverifiable numbers**
   - Galat: ek banaya-hua "₹2 CRORE" jo video mein kahin nahi aata.
   - Fix: sirf wahi numbers use karo jo tum sach mein dikhate ho, ya non-numeric hook par switch karo (**CHHUPE CHARGES**, **SECRET RULE**).

> Publish karne se pehle one-line gut check: *"Agar koi regulator sirf is thumbnail ka screenshot le, to kya main har word aur number defend kar sakta hoon?"* Agar nahi, to dobara likho.
